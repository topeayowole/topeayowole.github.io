About Us

Mission

Redeemer's Men Fellowship is a Christ Oriented Ministry for men, by men who through the gospel of Jesus Christ emphasize the biblical importance of family, encourage personal growth, and reach out through love, prayer and fellowship.

Scope

From the mission statement, it is obvious that "the men" in church are the focus. He functions in different capacities in life.

  1. Man as a father
  2. The man as a husband
  3. The man as the breadwinner
  4. The man as spiritual head of his family
  5. The man and his spiritual walk
  6. The man and his finances
  7. The man and his health

Structure

The following structure is expected to be employed to administer the activities of the Fellowship at all levels; Regional, Provincial, Zonal, Area and Parish.

  • President
  • Vice President
  • Secretary
  • Assistant Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Financial Secretary
  • Public Relations Officer

Reporting Line

Recognition is given to the strategic rule of the Province in ensuring the smooth operation of the Fellowship at all levels. The Pastor in-Charge of the Province therefore, has the responsibility for appointing the President of the Fellowship at the Province level and also approves the appointment of the position at other levels.
